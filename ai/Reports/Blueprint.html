The system requires a controlled transition to live usage with continuous monitoring.

Phase Goal Retraining Trigger
Phase 1: Paper Trading (Virtual) Confirm $\text{Sharpe Ratio}$ ($\approx 7.25$) holds up against **live market data** and **real-time execution friction** for 3-6 months. If the $\text{Paper Trading Sharpe Ratio}$ drops below $5.0$ for two consecutive months, the model **must be retrained** using the latest data ($\text{Walk-Forward}$ Optimization).
Phase 2: Live Monitoring Continuously track for $\text{Model Drift}$ (degradation of prediction accuracy) and $\text{Feature Drift}$ (changes in input data distribution).
